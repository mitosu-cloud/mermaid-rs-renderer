## Basic Timeline

```mermaid
timeline
    title History of Social Media Platform
    2002 : LinkedIn
    2004 : Facebook
         : Google
    2005 : Youtube
    2006 : Twitter
```

timeline

    section 17th-20th century
        Industry 1.0 : Machine industry
        Industry 2.0 : Resistance line
    section 21st century

        Industry 4.0 : Future industry
